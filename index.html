Who We Are

About VEON MotorSports

VEON MotorSports is a driver development company focused on building complete racing drivers through a structured, disciplined, and realistic progression model. Our work is centered on high-performance shifter karting as the foundation, followed by a guided transition into open-wheel formula cars such as Formula Enterprises 2 (FE2) and FIA Formula 4 (F4).

We do not sell championships or shortcuts. We develop drivers through process, education, and exposure to the correct competitive environments at the right time.

Our Approach

Development Philosophy

Process Over Results

Lap time improvement, technical understanding, racecraft, and mental discipline are prioritized over trophies.

Shifter Karting as a Foundation

Shifter karts provide unmatched training in braking, race starts, car control, and mechanical feedback.

Realistic Career Mapping

Each driver follows a personalized path based on ability, maturity, budget efficiency, and long-term goals.

Core Training

Shifter Karting Focus

Shifter karting is the core of our program. It is the most direct and cost-effective environment to prepare drivers for professional-level car racing.

Technical Skills

  • High-speed braking techniques
  • Gear management and downshifting
  • Data analysis and driver feedback
  • Chassis tuning fundamentals

Competitive Environment

  • National and regional shifter championships
  • High-grip, high-pressure race formats
  • Multi-session race weekends
Career Path

Transition to Formula Cars

Moving from karting to cars is not a jump it is a transition. VEON MotorSports prepares drivers technically, physically, and mentally before entering open-wheel competition.

1

Advanced Shifter Mastery

Consistent pace, race wins, mechanical understanding, and mental control.

2

Formula Car Testing

Introductory testing in FE2 or F4 to understand aerodynamics, braking systems, and car feedback.

3

Structured Championship Entry

Selective championship participation focused on learning, not immediate results.
